Ingredients:
 - 1 packet of maggie
 - 1 cup water
 - 2 chopped potatoes
 - 1 small bowl peas
 - 1 chopped carrot
 - 2 tbsp oil
 - 1 tbsp salt

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, heat oil.
  2. Add the chopped poatoes, carrot and the bowl of peas to it.
  3. Add 1 tbsp of salt and heat till done.
  3. Add maggie and maggie masala to the vegetables with a cup of water.
  4. Stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve it hot.
